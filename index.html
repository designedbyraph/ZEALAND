About Zealand Consult

Introduction

Zealand management consultancy limited was incorporated to provide outsourced labor services to companies in Nigeria, as well as, professional support services for small and medium enterprises to enable them focus on more creative and client-facing aspects of growing the business.

The Company

Zealand Management Consultancy limited was incorporated in March, 2012 to provide consultancy, Personnel recruitments/outsourced labor management and training with view to stimulating the effectiveness of organization through the provision of support services. The company has assembled a team of experienced and energetic professionals who provide effective, reliable and cutting edge style recruitments/outsourcing services to meet and exceed the expectations of our clients in order for them to remain competitive. We pride ourselves in exceptional partnership, excellence service delivery unbeatable innovation and unwavering integrity. Zealand management consultancy limited has capacities in human resource management, cleaning, training and industrial relations.

Vision

To be adjudged as an innovative, effective and one stop HR solutions partner

Mission

We are a highly ethical HR solutions organization with a professional and strategic team. We provide quality, cost effective and seamless outsourcing recruitment and HR advisory services that support our clients in becoming productive, profitable and globally competitive.

Our Philosophy

Our business is trust, quality, service, flexibility, ethic and professionalism. By keeping our clients in focus, we start adding value once we begin the relationship, with dynamic quality service and upmost professionalism.

Services

We offer focused HR solutions to meet your needs.

Outsourced Labor Management

End-to-end outsourced labour management to help you scale operations without the HR overhead. Recruitment, onboarding, supervision and payroll support so your team can focus on core activities.

Human Resource Advisory Services

Strategic HR advisory — policy design, performance management, retention strategies, and training solutions tailored for small and medium enterprises.
